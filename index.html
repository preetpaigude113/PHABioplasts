About PHA Bioplasts

PHA Bioplasts is a material-innovation venture that converts segregated hospitality and QSR organic waste into Polyhydroxyalkanoate (PHA) — a high-performance, fully biodegradable polymer for packaging and disposables. We pair waste valorization with product-grade engineering to deliver compostable, food-safe, and export-ready packaging solutions.

Technology

Nature-powered biochemical conversion, optimized for performance and circularity.

Feedstock Collection

Segregated food waste from partner hotels, cafes & QSRs.

Microbial Fermentation

Microbes convert organic carbon into intracellular PHA.

Extraction & Pellets

Purified PHA pellets produced for molding and extrusion.

Products

Premium compostable solutions for hospitality, delivery, and FMCG brands.

PHA Cutlery

Heat-resistant, sturdy, and compostable cutlery for hotels & catering.

PHA Carry Bags

Durable, flexible carry bags suitable for retail & takeaway operations.

Containers & Films

Leak-resistant containers and moisture-barrier films for food and FMCG.
