About PHA Bioplasts

PHA Bioplasts is a material-innovation venture converting hospitality food waste into PHA resin and biodegradable packaging. Our system creates measurable ESG impact for hotels, QSRs, cloud kitchens & FMCG brands.

Technology

Nature-powered conversion with real circularity impact.

Feedstock Collection

Segregated organic waste from hotels & QSRs.

Microbial Fermentation

Optimized strains convert organic carbon to PHA.

Extraction & Pellets

High-quality PHA resin suitable for molding & films.

Products

Premium compostable packaging engineered for performance.
