About PHA Bioplasts

PHA Bioplasts is building a circular system for food-service packaging. We collect segregated organic waste from hotels, cafés and QSRs, convert it biologically into PHA resin, and then manufacture compostable, high-performance packaging products.

What we solve

We address two issues simultaneously: unmanaged hospitality food waste and the dependence on conventional plastics for everyday packaging and disposables.

How we deliver

By placing modular PHA production close to waste sources, we keep logistics short, ensure consistent feedstock, and offer partners a clear shift to certified compostable materials.

Technology — from waste to PHA

Our process is built on established PHA biotechnology, adapted for hospitality waste streams and packaging-grade outputs.

1. Feedstock handling

Segregated organic waste is collected from partner hotels and QSRs. Moisture, contaminants and composition are monitored so that each batch is suitable for fermentation.

  • Source-segregated food waste
  • Basic pre-processing (shredding, homogenisation)
  • Traceable batches from each partner

2. Microbial fermentation

Specialised microorganisms consume the carbon in the waste stream and store it as PHA granules inside their cells. Process parameters are tuned for stability and yield.

  • Controlled pH and aeration
  • Optimised carbon-to-nutrient ratios
  • Scalable bioreactor design

3. Extraction & pelletising

After fermentation, the biomass is processed to extract PHA, which is purified and converted into pellets or granules suitable for standard plastic processing equipment.

  • PHA resin tailored for molding & films
  • Formulations tuned for stiffness/flexibility
  • Designed for compostability and food contact

Products & applications

Our PHA materials are converted into packaging and food-service products designed specifically for hospitality, delivery and FMCG brands.

Food Containers & Films

Rigid containers, lids and flexible films for food packaging and FMCG use-cases.
